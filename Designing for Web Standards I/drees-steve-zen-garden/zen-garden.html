Zen Garden

This page shows you a list of three of my favorite css designs from CSS Zen Garden.

Ocean Garden

I chose the Ocean Garden css layout because it is very appealing to the eyes. If you would like to view it click here.

  • The background picture is soothing and reminds me of a nice weekend at the beach.
  • The white and orange text on a blue background, makes it very easy to read.
  • The sections of text are aligned and consolidated for a quick and simple read.

Withering Beauty

I chose the Withering Beauty layout because of the details which reminds me of a family holiday. If you would like to view it click here.

  • While some of the headings in orange are hard to read. The color of the headings fits perfectly with the layout, in my opinion.
  • The background photo gives a warm feeling of a family holiday, get together.
  • The icon's that separate the paragraphs, give it a smooth flow when reading.

Museum

I chose the Museum layout because of the simplicity and flow of the design. If you would like to view it click here.

  • The way that the paragraphs are sectioned to look like they are mounted in a museum makes it really easy to see the separation and creates a smooth flowing design.
  • The three colors black, white and grey help portray a smooth flowing design.
  • I like the way that the first letter of every paragraph is a different font and font size. I makes it easier to note which section is which.
